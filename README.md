# FundForge – A Smart Platform for Getting Investment and Investing
FundForge is a modern web-based platform that bridges the gap between innovators and investors. It empowers startups, entrepreneurs, and project creators to showcase their ideas and receive funding support from interested investors — all in a secure, transparent, and user-friendly environment.

## Project Overview

In today’s digital era, finding the right investors or projects to fund can be challenging. FundForge acts as a smart crowdfunding and investment platform where:

Founders can post their ideas/projects with detailed information and funding goals.

Investors can browse, evaluate, and invest in projects that match their interests.

Both parties benefit from a centralized, secure, and interactive system that promotes trust and innovation.

## Key Features

✅ User Authentication & Role Management – Separate access for investors and founders.
✅ Project Creation & Listing – Founders can add project details, images, and goals.
✅ Investment Matching – Investors can explore and invest in suitable startups.
✅ Real-Time Dashboard – Insightful stats, funding progress, and personalized data.
✅ Secure Transactions – Simulated or real payment integration (as per phase).
✅ Notifications & Updates – Keeps both sides informed about new opportunities.
✅ Responsive UI/UX – Built with a modern, mobile-friendly interface.
✅ Scalable Architecture – Built with maintainability and performance in mind.
